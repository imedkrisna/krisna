The event opened with remarks from the editor and the director of research and studies at PPID, as well as a keynote speech from Professor (Emeritus) Dorodjatun Kuntjoro-Jakti.

The event continued with a panel discussion. The panelists were the Director of Population and Social Security, Dr. Muhammad Cholifihani, Unpad lecturer and TNP2K researcher Dr. Raden Purnagunawan, and two book authors, Malik Cahyadin and Hildayanti Loekman.

The event can be viewed on the [PPI Dunia YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7Dt-ZFjEfx8).
